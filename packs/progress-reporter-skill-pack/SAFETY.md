# Safety Rules

## Allowed

- Summarize completed public or user-provided work.
- Translate technical logs into plain-language progress.
- Prepare platform-safe buyer updates.
- Identify missing inputs, blockers, and realistic next steps.

## Not Allowed

- Faking progress, tests, screenshots, income, orders, or customer activity.
- Asking buyers to leave the platform.
- Opening or encouraging suspicious buyer links.
- Handling payment, payout, tax, KYC, wallet, subscription, or identity tasks.
- Revealing private file paths, tokens, cookies, emails, or credentials.

## Escalate To User

Escalate when the next step requires:

- Buyer approval.
- Payment, payout, tax, subscription, or KYC action.
- OAuth or high-permission authorization.
- Uploading personal identity documents.
- Access to private repositories or private customer systems.
