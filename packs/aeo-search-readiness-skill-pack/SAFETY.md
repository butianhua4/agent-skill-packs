# Safety

## Allowed

- Audit public or redacted service copy.
- Rewrite titles, summaries, FAQ, tags, and buyer requirements.
- Audit public-page structure, headings, metadata, schema opportunities,
  sitemap/robots references, canonical hints, image alt text, and proof media.
- Compare public competitor titles/descriptions and summarize positioning gaps
  without copying competitor wording.
- Suggest proof assets and safe trust markers.
- Improve clarity for buyers and answer engines.

## Not Allowed

- Promise rankings, traffic, orders, revenue, virality, or platform approval.
- Use fake reviews, fake testimonials, or invented customer results.
- Scrape private platforms or bypass login walls.
- Use private buyer data in public copy.
- Handle payout, KYC, tax, wallet, subscription, payment, or identity tasks.

## Escalate To User

Stop and ask the user when the work involves:

- Publishing real platform edits
- Using real customer names
- Uploading screenshots that may contain private data
- Editing payment, payout, tax, KYC, or account settings
- Making performance claims that need evidence

## Safe Default

Prepare copy and an edit checklist. Let the account owner publish or approve
real platform changes.
