# Content Distribution Skill

## Purpose

Convert one technical or service asset into safe, platform-ready distribution
copy. Use this to turn GitHub repos, releases, demos, Fiverr gigs, portfolio
proof, or buyer-safe case studies into publishable content for multiple channels.

## Inputs

Accept one or more of:

- GitHub README, release note, demo page, or commit summary
- Fiverr gig title, package, gallery asset, FAQ, or buyer-safe proof
- Skill-pack description, workflow, safety boundary, or delivery template
- Redacted client story, sample report, or before/after result
- Target platform list and preferred language

Reject or ask for redaction when inputs contain:

- Passwords, tokens, cookies, secret values, payout details, KYC material, or ID
  documents
- Private buyer data, private repositories, unreleased private code, or sensitive
  screenshots
- Requests to auto-post, mass-message, scrape users, or contact buyers outside a
  platform's rules

## Workflow

1. Extract the core asset:
   - Problem solved
   - Who it helps
   - Proof or demo
   - Safety boundary
   - Call to action
2. Choose the distribution angle:
   - Problem/solution
   - Before/after
   - Mini tutorial
   - Build-in-public update
   - Buyer proof
   - Community contribution
3. Generate platform-specific drafts:
   - GitHub release/update: concise technical summary
   - Fiverr profile/gig update: buyer outcome and scope
   - OpenClaw/community post: contribution, usage, and safety notes
   - X: short hook, proof, and link placeholder
   - LinkedIn: professional context, lesson, and business outcome
   - Xiaohongshu: practical story, 9-card outline, and searchable tags
   - Xianyu/private group: buyer pain, package, price anchor, and safe intake
4. Add reply hooks:
   - "What should I send?"
   - "Can you fix it directly?"
   - "How much?"
   - "Can you log in?"
   - "Can you guarantee?"
5. Produce a publishing order and reuse plan.

## Output

Return a concise Markdown bundle:

- Asset summary
- Recommended angle
- Platform draft table
- Full drafts for the top 3 channels
- 9-card Xiaohongshu / image-card outline when relevant
- Buyer-safe reply hooks
- Publishing checklist

Use this table:

| Platform | Goal | Draft type | CTA | Safety note |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

## Boundaries

- Do not auto-publish or operate platform accounts without explicit user action.
- Do not scrape audiences, mass-message strangers, or bypass anti-spam systems.
- Do not promise revenue, virality, ranking, orders, or platform approval.
- Do not ask for account login, payout, wallet, tax, payment, KYC, or identity
  handling.
- Keep private buyer details out of public posts.
- Prefer "proof + safe intake" over hype.
