# Market Opportunity Radar Skill Pack

Turn public market notes, trend snippets, buyer comments, or competitor
observations into ranked service opportunities and next actions.

This pack is designed for safe, buyer-facing market analysis. It does not scrape
private platforms, bypass login walls, or claim guaranteed revenue.

## Best For

- Fiverr or domestic platform service ideation
- AI agent / automation product discovery
- Turning daily market notes into sellable offer ideas
- Prioritizing which gig, skill pack, or demo to build next

## Output

- Opportunity table
- Buyer pain summary
- Service-package suggestion
- Risk and effort score
- Next asset to build
