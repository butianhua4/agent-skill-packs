# Safety

## Allowed

- Analyze public summaries, user-provided market notes, and redacted buyer
  messages.
- Rank opportunities by demand, urgency, deliverability, proof, and risk.
- Recommend safe service packages, buyer replies, and proof assets.

## Not Allowed

- Import cookies, sessions, tokens, or credentials.
- Scrape login-only platforms or bypass access controls.
- Open suspicious buyer links.
- Handle payout, KYC, tax, wallet, subscription, payment, or identity tasks.
- Guarantee earnings, ranking, platform approval, or viral performance.

## Escalate To Owner

Ask the owner before:

- Publishing a paid listing
- Replying to a real buyer
- Accepting a custom order
- Using private repositories or private customer data
- Touching identity, payout, payment, or tax settings
