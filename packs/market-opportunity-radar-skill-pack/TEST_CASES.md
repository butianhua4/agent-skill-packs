# Test Cases

## Test 1: Daily AI Market Notes

Input:

- AI Agent development demand is increasing
- Buyers ask for n8n/Zapier/Make automation fixes
- Generic content writing is declining
- AI skill packs and reusable workflows are trending

Expected:

- Rank workflow diagnosis, automation rescue, and skill-pack bundles above
  generic writing.
- Recommend one entry offer and one upsell.
- Include a safety boundary around account access and secrets.
