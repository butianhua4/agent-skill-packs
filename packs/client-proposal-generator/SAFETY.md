# Safety

Allowed:

- Draft proposals from public or user-provided requirements.
- Use budget ranges.
- Explain assumptions.
- Prepare platform-safe custom offer text.

Requires user confirmation:

- Final price
- Delivery deadline
- Scope commitments
- Private system access
- Any platform message before sending

Do not include:

- Off-platform payment instructions
- Credentials or secret requests
- False portfolio claims
- Guaranteed income or guaranteed business outcomes
