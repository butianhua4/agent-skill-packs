# Safety

## Allowed

- Convert public or redacted signals into service packages.
- Create fixed-scope pricing tiers, buyer requirements, and delivery boundaries.
- Prepare copy for Fiverr, GitHub, OpenClaw/community, and domestic platforms.
- Suggest proof assets that can be created from public demos or redacted samples.

## Not Allowed

- Guarantee income, orders, marketplace ranking, approvals, or viral reach.
- Ask buyers for passwords, cookies, tokens, secret values, IDs, or payment data.
- Handle payout, KYC, tax, wallet, subscription, payment, or identity tasks.
- Use private buyer data in a public listing or proof asset.
- Publish listings or send custom offers without user confirmation.

## Escalate To User

Stop and ask the user when the task involves:

- Real platform publishing
- Real buyer messaging
- Payment or payout setup
- Identity verification
- Private repos or private buyer material
- Claims about guaranteed business results

## Safe Default

Create a draft offer and proof checklist. Keep the final publish/send step with
the account owner.
