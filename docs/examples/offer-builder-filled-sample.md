# Filled Sample: Offer Builder Plan

This sample uses a fictional service idea. It is designed as buyer-facing proof
for the `offer-builder-skill-pack`.

## Offer Plan: AI Automation Failure Diagnosis

### Offer Decision

Publish as a low-friction entry offer, then use diagnosis results to route
qualified buyers into a scoped repair-plan upgrade.

### Buyer And Pain

- Buyer: founder, operator, no-code builder, or small team using AI automation
- Trigger event: workflow broke, cost spiked, agent looped, or tool calls failed
- Pain: they do not know whether the issue is prompt logic, JSON mapping, tool
  configuration, missing data, or unsafe retry behavior
- Why now: broken automation blocks operations and creates support or delivery
  delays

### Package Ladder

| Tier | Buyer problem | Deliverable | Price band | Timeline | Upgrade path |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Entry | "I need to know why it failed." | Redacted log diagnosis report | USD 49-99 | 24h | Repair plan |
| Standard | "I know the failure and need a fix plan." | Scoped repair plan with validation steps | USD 199-399 | 2-3 days | Implementation |
| Premium | "Multiple workflows are unreliable." | Workflow risk map, fix plan, and handoff templates | USD 599+ | 5-7 days | Custom support |

### Title Options

1. I will diagnose your broken AI automation workflow and deliver a clear fix plan
2. I will debug your AI agent logs and find the failed tool or data handoff
3. I will audit your n8n, Zapier, or AI agent workflow failure safely

### One-Line Offer

I will inspect your redacted automation logs or screenshots and deliver a clear
failure map, likely root cause, and safe next-step fix plan.

### Buyer Requirements

- Redacted error message or screenshot
- Redacted sample input/output
- Tool or platform name
- What changed before the failure
- Desired successful outcome
- No passwords, tokens, cookies, IDs, payment details, or private customer data

### Delivery Scope

Included:

- Failure category
- Likely root cause
- Risk level
- Fix recommendation
- Validation checklist
- Buyer-safe delivery summary

Excluded:

- Login/account operation
- Payment, payout, tax, wallet, KYC, or identity work
- Guaranteed revenue, ranking, or production outcome
- Direct production changes without a separate scoped implementation order

### FAQ / Objection Replies

- Price concern: "The entry offer is for diagnosis only, so you know the failure
  before paying for a larger repair."
- Direct-fix request: "I can scope a repair after the diagnosis; I do not change
  production systems inside the diagnosis package."
- Login request: "Please send redacted screenshots, errors, and sample payloads.
  I do not need account access for the diagnosis."
- Guarantee request: "I can identify likely causes and safe fixes from the
  materials provided, but I cannot guarantee platform behavior or production
  outcomes."
- Vague inquiry: "Send the error, platform name, sample input/output, and what
  should have happened. I will tell you whether it fits the diagnosis package."
