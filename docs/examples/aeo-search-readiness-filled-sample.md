### Rewritten Blocks

#### Title Options

1. I will debug failed AI agent workflow logs and deliver a fix plan
2. I will diagnose your broken AI automation and find the root cause
3. I will audit failed n8n, Zapier, or AI agent logs safely

#### One-Line Summary

Send redacted errors, screenshots, or sample payloads and I will return a clear
failure map, likely root cause, safe fix scope, and validation checklist.

#### Opening Paragraph

If your AI agent or automation workflow failed and the logs are hard to read, I
will inspect your redacted materials and turn them into a clear diagnosis report.
You will receive the failed step, likely root cause, risk level, recommended fix,
and validation checklist so you know what to repair next.

#### Recommended Heading Outline

- H1: AI Agent Workflow Debugging Report
- H2: What I Can Diagnose
- H2: What You Need To Send
- H2: What You Receive
- H3: Safe Boundaries And Upgrade Path

#### FAQ

Q: What do I need to send?
A: Redacted error messages, screenshots, sample input/output, platform name, and
what should have happened.

Q: Can you log in and fix it directly?
A: This diagnosis package does not require account login. If implementation is
needed, it should be scoped separately after the report.

Q: Can you guarantee results?
A: I can provide a clear diagnosis and recommended validation steps based on the
materials you provide, but I cannot guarantee third-party platform behavior or
production outcomes.
